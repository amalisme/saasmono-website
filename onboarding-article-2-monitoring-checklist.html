üéØ How to Prioritize Your Monitors

Can't monitor everything? Start with these priorities

üî¥ PRIORITY 1: Revenue-Generating Pages

  • ‚Ä¢ SaaS: Login, signup, dashboard
  • ‚Ä¢ Service: Booking page, contact form
  • ‚Ä¢ Content: Highest-traffic articles with ads

üü° PRIORITY 2: Customer-Facing Pages

üü¢ PRIORITY 3: Nice-to-Have Pages

üìã Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1 Make a List
