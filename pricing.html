Pricing Questions

Can I really use the free plan forever?

Yes, absolutely. There's no trial period or forced upgrade. The free plan is a legitimate offering that many small businesses use long-term.

What if I need more than 50 monitors but don't want to pay?

The free plan limit is 50 monitors, which covers most small businesses. If you need more, the Pro plan at $7/month gives you unlimited monitors—still very affordable.

Is 5-minute checking enough?

For most small businesses, yes. It means maximum 5 minutes of undetected downtime. If you need faster detection (1-minute checks), the Pro plan is worth considering.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no contracts or commitments. You can downgrade to free or cancel paid plans anytime without penalty.

What payment methods do you accept?

UptimeRobot accepts all major credit cards, PayPal, and various other payment methods depending on your region.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes, annual billing typically offers 2 months free compared to monthly billing. Check their site for current offers.
